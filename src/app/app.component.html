Brief School presentation

The ENSET of Kumba was created on 7 March 2014 by Decree No. 2014/090 of the President of the Republic after the announcement was made by the latter a few days earlier at the celebration of the fiftieth anniversary of the Reunification in Buea . Shortly thereafter, the decree of opening was intervened and since then, the ENSET of Kumba is functional. The Higher Technical Teaching Training College (HTTTC) in Kumba is an institution of the University of Buea
